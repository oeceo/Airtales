About airtales

Airtales is a daily journal platform designed to spark creativity, encourage reflection, and connect people through shared storytelling. Every day, users around the world receive a single word prompt, inviting them to share experiences, express themselves, and capture a moment in time through writing. No two interpretations are ever the same, making each day a unique and personal journey.

What makes Airtales different?

To keep the focus on the writing and to remove some of the barriers to sharing openly and creatively online, the identity of each author is hidden from other users. Posts are only shared with a location to appear on the explore map, which allows our community to see how the prompt has been interpreted in different places. Users can show their appreciation by liking posts and can report posts that don't align with our community's terms, but beyond that we let the writing do the talking.
