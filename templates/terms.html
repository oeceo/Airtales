2. Purpose

Airtales is an experimental platform created for our Internet Technology class. It allows users to practice journalling by responding to the daily prompt with a short entry. All posts are shared publicly along with a location pin but no username, encouraging users to focus on the writing and exploring how the prompt has been interpreted around the world.
